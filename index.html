Making your coffee

Featuring the freshest in our expanded range of seasonal coffees sourced throughout the year. Always serve your coffee fresh, using filtered soft water at a temperature of 92C. Saturate the grounds in the press at a coffee:water ratio of 1:15. After five minutes, skim the crust of the surface and allow to stand for a further two minutes. Plunge delicately, serve hot, and enjoy!
